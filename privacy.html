privacy.

last updated 14 july 2026.

the short version

you gave us your email so we could tell you when a quit-vaping app exists. that is the only thing we will use it for.

what we collect

your email address, and the rough details your browser hands over automatically (the type of device/browser you used to sign up). nothing else. we don't ask for your name, we don't run creepy trackers, we don't need to know how much you vape.

what we do with it

we store it securely so we can send you one email when the app launches, and possibly a small number of updates before then. that's it. we do not sell it, rent it, or hand it to advertisers or vape companies (several of whom would love it, which is exactly why they can't have it).

where it lives

your email is stored in our database (hosted by supabase, on servers in the eu). it's protected so that the public website can add your address but can't read the list back out.

getting off the list

every email we send has an unsubscribe link. you can also just reply and tell us to delete you, or write to hello@vapid.app, and we'll remove your address for good.

your rights

if you're in the uk/eu, you have the right to access, correct, or delete the data we hold about you, and to complain to your data protection authority. email us and we'll sort it.

this is a plain-language summary for a pre-launch waitlist and isn't legal advice. it'll be replaced with a fuller policy when the app ships. questions: hello@vapid.app.
